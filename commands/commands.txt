Prefix: ! Usage: `!(Command|Alias) <Required> [Optional]`

General Commands:

  !roll <chosen dice> [number of rolls] - Rolls the selected die and returns the value rolled.
    valid arguments: d4, d6, d8, d12, d20, percent
    example: !roll d4 3
    output: @User rolled: 2, 1, 4

  !coinflip - Flips a coin and returns heads or tails
    example: !coinflip
    output: @User's coin was HEADS
